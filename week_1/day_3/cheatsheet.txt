Continuous:Variables that can take on any numerical value 
    (histograms/scatter plots)
Discrete: Count of a Characteristic,result,itme or action. positive Integers not divided into smaller increments
    (box plot, bar chart) 
Categorical:Data that fits into categories. Interested in ratios and proportions of this data.
    (stacked Bar Chart, Pie Chart)
Nominal: Categorical data with no inherent order or structure (gender,hair,eye,nationality)
    (bar,pie chart or histograms)
Ordinal: Categorical data with an inherent order or structure (educatioj level,shirt size,rating scales)
    bar charts histogrrams or line charts
Binary Data: true or false , 
    (Bar Chart or Pie chart)

Qualitative Data:
    descriptor data, non numerical data (opinions feelings beliefs or attitudes)

Quantitative Data:
    Numerical Data measurements  or counts.(height,weight,age,income,test scores)
